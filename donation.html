Make a Difference Today

There are multiple ways you can assist Food For Life South Africa

1) Sign up for a Monthly Debit Order

This is the most popular option and helps sustain our daily feeding efforts. For more information or to request a debit order form, please email info@fflsa.org.

2) Donate Online

This can be done via our secure online portal. Simply enter the amount you wish to donate and proceed. Donation can be made via credit card, debit card or EFT.

3) Donate Food Items

Food items such as rice, oil, and frozen vegetables can be donated at Food For Life branches. Contact your nearest branch to arrange this. A list of our branches is available in the main menu.

4) Volunteering

Volunteer your time to assist with food preparation, cooking, and distribution. Other skills like marketing, IT, and finance are also appreciated. Please contact your nearest branch or email info@fflsa.org for more information.

5) Monthly Donations for International Donors
