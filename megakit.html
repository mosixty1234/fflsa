Centre Road Mega Kitchen Project

Located at 2 Centre Road, Newlands, Food For Life‚Äôs New Mega Kitchen will serve the greater Durban area, providing free, fresh, nutritious meals to communities and individuals. The kitchen will have the capacity of producing 4000 hot fresh meals each day.

Food For Life is a section 18A registered NPO and donations made towards this project are tax deductible.

Food For Life is 95% volunteer run and does not receive any government funding. Feeding is made possible by generous donations from the general public. We appeal to the public to support this project.
