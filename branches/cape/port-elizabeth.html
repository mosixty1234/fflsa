Latest News

🍽️ Feeding at Klein Skool

On 20th November 2015, our Food For Life team carried out a food distribution at Klein Skool, hosted at St. Paul’s Catholic Church.

This initiative was made possible through the generous sponsorship of the Manga Family. Their kindness and support helped us bring warmth and nourishment to those in need.

We are deeply grateful to all our wonderful donors, who open their hearts to assist us in making a difference in the lives of others. ❤️

🍽️ Feeding in Zwide

On 18th November 2015, our Food For Life team conducted a food distribution in Zwide, bringing nourishment to the community.

This initiative was generously sponsored by the Sooraj Family, whose support made it possible to serve warm and comforting meals.

Hot Kitchri was lovingly prepared and served to everyone, spreading joy and care. We sincerely appreciate our donors for their kindness in helping us make a difference. ❤️
